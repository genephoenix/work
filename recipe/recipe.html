Recipe: Delicious Chicken Curry

A variation on South Indian chicken curry.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 2 cinnamon sticks (broken into little pieces)
  • A few curry leaves (optional)
  • 2-3 large onions
  • 1 green chili pepper (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 3 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 1 potato
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinammon

Note: These are only some suggested amounts. Feel free to increase/decrease the amount of spices, onions,tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste or chicken to match your own tastes.

Prep Work

  1. Wash the chicken and vegetables.
  2. Cut the chicken into small- medium sized cubes and set aside on a plate.
  3. Dice the onions and then chop the potato and tomatoes into cubes. Place them all into separate bowls and set to the side.

Directions

  1. Add oil to the pan on the stove and set the temperature to medium.

  2. Once the pan has heated up enough, add in the curry leaves, cardamom pods, whole cloves and cinammon sticks. After about a minute, add the onions to the pan.

  3. Wait until the onions turn a translucent shade and then add in the ginger-garlic paste. Stir well.

  4. Once the paste starts showing signs of sticking to the pan, add in the tomatoes and turn the heat to low. Place the lid onto the pan and wait until the tomatoes soften.

  5. Next, add in the potatoes and cook with cover on for ~5 minutes.

  6. Afterwards, add in the chicken, topping it with your turmeric and chili powder. Cook on medium heat for ~5 minutes without cover on and then, once the chicken shows signs of being lightly cooked on all sides,replace the pan top and cook on low heat for another 5 minutes.

  7. Now toss in some water covering up the chicken and potatoes and then cover the pan once again. Lastly, after a few minutes, add in the ground spices and place the lid on again, waiting until the potatoes are soft (~ 5-7 minutes)

  8. Finally it's all ready to eat!

Best enjoyed with some naan bread or rice!
